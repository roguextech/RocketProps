
.. n2o_prop

N2O
===


`N2O <http://www.astronautix.com/n/n2o.html>`_ or
Liquid nitrous oxide (N2O / dinitrogen monoxide / 'laughing gas') is the oxidizer 
of choice for hybrid rocket motors because it is benign, storable, and 
self-pressurizing.
